About Us


Our Passion - Love and care for all cats and dogs

At Seb’s Grooming, we believe that every pet deserves to look and feel their best. Our passion for animals drives us to provide the highest level of care and attention during every grooming session. Whether your furry friend is here for a simple bath or a full spa day, we make sure they’re treated with love, patience, and respect.

Experienced Groomers - Professional, Trained, Gentle Handlers

Our team consists of skilled and certified pet groomers with years of experience working with dogs, cats, and other small pets. We understand that grooming can be stressful for some pets, so we use calm handling techniques and positive reinforcement to ensure a safe and pleasant experience.

Safe & Comfortable Environment

We’ve designed our grooming space to make pets feel at home — clean, cozy, and filled with gentle energy. Our salon is equipped with professional-grade grooming tools and products that are safe for all breeds and skin types. Hygiene and comfort are our top priorities, ensuring your pet leaves looking great and feeling happy.
